The bill aims to establish a program to provide grants for training in the field of cybersecurity, with a focus on underrepresented groups. It also seeks to create a scholarship program for students pursuing degrees in cybersecurity. Additionally, the bill includes provisions for the establishment of a Cybersecurity Advisory Board to advise on program implementation and evaluation. Furthermore, it proposes the development of a national cybersecurity workforce strategy to address current and future workforce needs in the field. Overall, the bill aims to enhance cybersecurity education and training opportunities to meet the growing demand for skilled professionals in the field.
